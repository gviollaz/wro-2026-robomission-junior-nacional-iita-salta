# Competition - WRO RoboMission Junior 2026

See Spanish version for full details: `docs/es/competition/README.md`

- **Theme:** Robots Meet Culture
- **Challenge:** Heritage Heroes (historic fortress)
- **Age:** 11-15 | **Max score:** 230 pts | **Max run time:** 2 min

## Missions
1. Guide visitors to colored areas (40 pts)
2. Rebuild towers - red (30 pts) + yellow stacking (50 pts)
3. Bring artefacts to museum (60 pts)
4. Clear cobblestone of dirt (20 pts)
5. Bonus - don't damage barriers/parrot (30 pts)

Randomization: 4 of 5 artefacts + 10 dirt particles random per round.
